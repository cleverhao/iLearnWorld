# 知识天地
知识天地是一个能学到各种各样的知识的网站，也感谢您对知识天地的支持与鼓励!
### 知识天地主页
知识天地是一款免费的能够用来学习的网站。它的主页分为三块内容： 文章、小百科、作者。打开列表便能快速浏览大纲，并来到所选内容区域。 也可以点击反馈/联系，用于联系作者/制作者哦！ 文章 的内容主要是大面积内容的学习，如：圣诞节，可以包含节日的起源、 圣诞老人和圣诞树的知识。但是并不是非常具体。要看很细致化的内容， 小百科绝对是最佳选择。 作者这一部分并不只是包含作者的内容、 信息，还包含对广大朋友们提出问题的回答和一些生活方面的文章。 首页的每一篇文章题目上都会注上标签。 Hot标签表示是观看的人多的； New标签表示是最新发布的； Good标签表示是经审核后非常好的。 大家通过这些标签，就可以从中挑选自己更加心仪的文章啦！
### 知识天地内容
知识天地内容分为文章/小百科/作者三部分，其中的横线会分割出不同的内容 （不包括二级/三级），每一部分的开头都会写着小标题。还有， 内容里的大部分内容都是从百度百科里搜出来的，如果有改动， 会在文章末尾显示出来哦！不过，作者也是要休息哒！ 我们有时会把一部分内容放在网站里，但一定会在文末提供查找百度的渠道哦！ 想看完整内容或比较喜欢百度百科风格的浏览，都可以翻到文末查看哦！ 文末也会显示出处、制作者和日期。
### 知识天地反馈/联系/投稿
知识天地反馈/联系的原理是发送邮件。发送邮件也分为两个部分， 可以发送给不同的作者/制作者邮件。发送到的邮件地址都会在按钮上面显示。 点击发送邮件，知识天地就会打开系统的邮件APP，如果打开失败， 请检查您的系统上是否有邮件APP，如果检查后发送仍然失败， 可以打开邮件APP直接按照上面的邮件地址发送给作者/制作者（可复制）。
### 知识天地后续
知识天地将会不断更新，满足各位客户的视觉和学习需求， 还会加进更多好玩而又新奇的功能，让您在本网站拥有更好的体验！
### 知识天地备注
知识天地比较适合在电脑端（PC端）运行，在移动端上可能会出现错位、字体大小 不同等现象。

#### 感谢您对知识天地的支持！


# iLearnWorld
iLearnWorld is a website where you can learn any thing, and you can contribute more to iLearnWorld!
### iLearnWorld Main Page
iLearnWorld is a free website that can be used to learn.Its main page is divided into three sections: articles, encyclopedias, and authors. Opening the list takes you quickly through the outline and brings you to the selected content area. You can also click Feedback/Contact to contact the author/producer!The content of the article is mainly to learn large areas of content, such as: Christmas, can include the origin of the holiday, Santa Claus and Christmas tree knowledge. But it's not very specific. For very detailed content, the encyclopedia is definitely the best choice.The author section not only contains the author's content, information, but also contains answers to the questions raised by the majority of friends and some articles on life. Each article on the front page will be labeled with a title. The "Hot" tag means more people are watching;The New tag indicates that it was recently published; The Good label means that it is very Good after review. Through these tags, you can choose your favorite articles from them!
### iLearnWorld content
The contents of iLearnWorld are divided into three sections: article/encyclopedia/author. The horizontal lines of the sections are divided into different sections (not including level 2 / level 3). Subtitles are written at the beginning of each section. Also, most of the content is from baidu encyclopedia search out, if there are changes, will be shown at the end of the article! However, the author also needs to rest, too(>-<)!We sometimes put a part of the content in the website, but will be in the end of the article to provide search baidu channels!Want to see the full content or more like baidu encyclopedia style browsing, can turn to the end of the article to view oh!The source, author and date are also shown at the end of the article.
### iLearnWorld feedback/contact/submisson
The principle of iLearnWorld feedback/contact/submission is to send an email. Sending mail is also divided into two parts, which can be sent to different authors/producers.The email address is displayed on the button. Click "Send Mail" and Knowledge World will open the email APP of the system. If it fails to open, please check whether there is an email APP on your system. If it still fails to send after checking, you can open the email APP and send it directly to the author/producer according to the above email address (can copy).

iLearnWorld will be constantly updated to meet the visual and learning needs of customers, but also will add more fun and novel functions, so that you have a better experience in this site!
